UNICEF @ Woodward

UNICEF is a service club at Woodward, dedicated to making a difference in the lives of children all around the world. By joining, you will learn more about UNICEF as an agency and what they do to help, educate yourself about global issues that specifically impact children in third world countries, earn service hours, and give back.
